Spreading Joy and Imagination

At JDEV, we are deeply committed to empowering people - our team, customers, and partners - as well as enriching the communities we're a part of. Through charitable donations, nonprofit collaborations, product contributions, and team member volunteerism, we strive to make the world a little brighter.

Central to this is our dedication to promoting creativity, self-expression, and confidence in all people. We support organizations that provide arts education and mentoring to underserved youth. We also partner with charities that assist women and girls in crisis to rediscover their inner light.

Fundamental to everything we do is our commitment to diversity, equity, and inclusion. We understand, appreciate, and celebrate the diversity of backgrounds, perspectives, and identities. Our outreach efforts represent this rich mixture.

We believe that by getting involved and giving back, we can spread more joy. Our team is dedicated to positively impacting lives - whether it's a smile from our products or through community action. At JDEV, we use our business as a force for imagination and good.
