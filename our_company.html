A Brand That Sparks and Wonder

JDEV is a makeup and lifestyle brand devoted to imagination, nostalgia, and self-expression. Our collections of vivid pastel cosmetics and dreamy accessories inspire customers to embrace their inner child and see the world through a softer, more playful lens.

Grounded in inclusivity, kindness, and creativity, JDEV comprises two distinct labels that share our core mission of spreading more lightness and joy. Our flagship JDEV line offers premium cosmetics and vintage-inspired fashions. While JDEV Mini caters to young creators through whimsical, affordable products designed to boost confidence.

We are committed to fostering a welcoming community for all dreamers, romantics, and free spirits. With a global team of over 200 associates, we aim to nurture personal growth and living life colorfully. Our goal is for customers to not only look good, but to feel good - playful, inspired, and uniquely themselves.

JDEV believes imagination has the power to uplift lives and create positive change. We will continue spreading our message of self-love and embracing your inner child through our products, content, and advocacy.
